About WBC College

Established in 2011 WBC college a renowned institution of higher learning. Institute offers 5 courses which are Degree in Full Time mode. Overall, Institute has diversified 4 UG courses and 1 PG course

Over the period, WBC college has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Physics, English.

Facilities provided

our college provides library,collection includes 16,713 books, both for borrowing and reference,well equiped classrooms that provides immersive learning

The college has a beautiful ambiance in sylvan surroundings. The college has an excellent academic record as students secure ranks in the university examination. The alumni of the college are serving at several institutions all over the country and abroad. Many are dynamic leaders of their institutions. Many are working in other government sectors.

Admission proess

All the admissions are subject to the approval of the college.

The college offers instructions in both English and French. Total intake is 100 seats respectively. The Central Admission Cell of the State under the Commissioner of Public Instruction shall monitor the admission to the student with scholarships.
